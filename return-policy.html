Retorne e reveja as politicas

At MaisonduTailleur, your satisfaction is our top priority. We understand that sometimes a purchase may not meet your expectations. That's why we've created a straightforward return and refund process to ensure your shopping experience is enjoyable.

1. retornar Eligibility

You can return most items within 30 days of receiving them for a full refund. To be eligible for a return, items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags still attached.

We encourage you to inspect your items upon arrival and report any issues as soon as possible to ensure a smooth return process.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are non-returnable, including but not limited to:

  • Gift cards
  • Final sala itens
  • Intimate apparel (for hygiene reasons)

Please check the product description for specific return eligibility.

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact our customer service team at maisondutailleur9@gmail.com. You will need to provide your order number and the reason for the return. We will send you a return authorization and a prepaid return shipping label via email.

Please ensure that the items are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit. You can drop off your package at the designated shipping location as instructed in the return label.

4. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it to ensure it meets our return criteria. We will process your refund within 5 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method you used for the purchase.

Please note that depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

5. Exchanges

If you need a different size or color, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired product. Unfortunately, we do not process direct exchanges.

To expedite the process, consider placing a new order before returning your original item to ensure availability.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

We strive for quality, but if you receive a damaged or defective item, please Contate-nos within 7 days of delivery. Provide your order number and a description of the issue, along with any supporting photos.

We will arrange for a replacement or refund based on your preference, at no additional cost to you.

7. International Returns

For customers outside of India, please note that you may be responsible for return shipping costs. Additionally, any customs duties or taxes incurred on the return will not be reimbursed.

To initiate an international return, contact our customer service for guidance on the return process.

8. Policy Updates

MaisonduTailleur reserves the right to update or modify this Return and Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the site will constitute acceptance of those changes.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.

Thank you for shopping with MaisonduTailleur. If you have any questions regarding our Return and Refund Policy, please don't hesitate to Contate-nos. We are here to assist you!
